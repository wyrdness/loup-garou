## Research Ethics & Cultural Sensitivity

When researching loup-garou/Rougarou/Lougarou traditions:

- **Respect living traditions** — This is active cultural heritage, not merely historical
- **Center Louisiana and Caribbean voices** — Prioritize Cajun, Creole, and Haitian scholars
- **Acknowledge colonial context** — Understand power dynamics and historical oppression
- **Avoid sensationalism** — Treat with academic seriousness and cultural respect
- **Protect informants** — Respect privacy of those sharing family traditions
- **Support communities** — Give back through education and cultural preservation
- **Linguistic respect** — Use French/Creole terminology appropriately
- **Recognize complexity** — Not simple entertainment but layered cultural phenomenon
